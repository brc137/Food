Easiest to access Restaurants

Easiest to access resuratunts is determined by the distance of the restaurant from towers as well as any additional obsicles that may hinder people from traveling to them, such as large hills

All of the easiest to access restaurants listed below are within 0.15 miles of Towers and the path to them is on relativly flat ground with no additional obsticals

0.15 mile radius from towers

  • Fuel and Fuddle
    • Type: american, sit down
    • Pricce: $10-$15
    • Speed of Service: 20-45 min depending on how busy
    • Dietary Testrictions: several vegetarian options, has options for gluten allergies.
    • Ease of Access: wheelchair accessible, 0.1 miles, about a 2 min walk, outdoor seating
  • India on Wheels
    • Type: Indian
    • Price: $6-$8
    • Speed of Service: 5-10 minutes (If during the last 10 mins of each hour... longer wait due to people walking to and from class)
    • Dietary Restricions: N/A
    • Ease of Access: 0.1mi flat surface walk
  • Thai Gourmet Food Truck
    • Type: Thai
    • Price: $10-$15
    • Speed of Service: 5-12 minutes (If during the last 10 mins of each hour... longer wait due to people walking to and from class)
    • Dietary Restricions: N/A
    • Ease of Access: 0.1mi flat surface walk
  • Chipotle
    • Type: Mexican
    • Price: $10-$15
    • Speed of Service: 3min-10min, depending on how busy
    • Dietary Restricions: there are options for gluten allergies and vegan
    • Ease of Access: wheelchair accessible. 0.13 miles(3min walk), indoor seating
  • Mala Hotpot
    • Type: Chinese
    • Price: $15+
    • Speed of Service: under 10min
    • Dietary Restricions: there are many options for food, and you choose what food you like, then they cook it for you.
    • Ease of Access: wheelchair accessible. 0.15miles(5min walk), indoor seating.
  • BAO
    • Type: Chinese
    • Price: $15-$20+
    • Speed of Service: 10-20 minutes
    • Dietary Restricions: there are several options for gluten allergies and vegan
    • Ease of Access: wheelchair accessible. 0.12miles(3-5min walk), indoor seating.
