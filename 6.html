Conjecture

Finding affordable and quick food options on a university campus can be a challenge, which is why we developed a solution to help students and other campus stakeholders easily locate the best food options that fit their needs and budget.

Our food finding system provides users with a list of available food options on campus, along with their prices and estimated wait times. By using our product, users can easily find affordable and quick food options that fit their preferences, without wasting time searching for options.

Definition

Our team worked together to identify the easiest to access food options, define what constitutes quickness, and establish what would be considered expensive. We used a variety of organizational practices such as scrums and communication methods to ensure everyone was on the same page.
